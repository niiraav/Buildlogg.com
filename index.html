How it works

From first call to paid,
in one place.

Three steps, no desk required. Buildlogg follows the job through its whole life — so nothing slips.

1

Quote on the spot

Standing in their hallway? Build a tidy quote in under a minute and send it before you've packed the toolbox.

2

Book & run the day

Accepted quotes become booked jobs with addresses, arrival windows and one-tap navigation. Open the app, see what's next.

3

Finish & get paid

Mark the job done. Record how it was paid, add any notes, and the job moves to complete. Your money-owed total updates itself.
