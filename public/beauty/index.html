How it works

From enquiry to
deposit, in one place.

Three steps, no desk required. Buildlogg follows the booking from first message to deposit paid — so nothing slips.

1

Book with a deposit

Got an enquiry? Send a booking link via WhatsApp. They pick a slot, pay a deposit, done. You both get confirmation instantly.

2

Remind automatically

Buildlogg sends a WhatsApp reminder 24 hours before. No-shows drop by half. Late cancels? The deposit stays with you.

3

Finish & get paid

Treatment done? Send the balance request in one tap. They pay. Your chair's free for the next client.
