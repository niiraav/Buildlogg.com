Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Buildlogg ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile app and website at buildlogg.com (the "Service").

Buildlogg is a job management tool for solo tradespeople and service providers. We're built on a simple principle: your business data is yours. We collect the minimum needed to run the Service and nothing more.

1. What we collect

Account information

  • Email address — used for login, account recovery, and important service notifications.
  • Business name, trade, and phone number — you provide these during onboarding. Your phone number is used so customers can reach you and so we can send you notifications about missed calls and job reminders.
  • Profile photo — optional, used on quotes and your public booking page if enabled.

Business data you create

  • Customer details — names, phone numbers, addresses, and email addresses you enter for your customers.
  • Jobs and quotes — job titles, descriptions, line items, pricing, dates, notes, and photos you upload.
  • Payment records — how and when a job was paid. If you use Stripe for card payments, payment is processed by Stripe — we store the payment method and status, not card numbers.
  • Message templates — templates you create for sending quotes and follow-ups via WhatsApp or SMS.

Usage data

  • Analytics — we use PostHog (EU-hosted) to understand which features are used and how the app performs. This is aggregate, anonymous data — we don't sell or share it.
  • Device and browser information — standard technical data collected automatically (device type, browser, app version).

2. How we use your data

  • To provide the Service — storing your jobs, customers, quotes, and payments.
  • To send quotes and messages on your behalf via WhatsApp or SMS (you initiate these; we don't send anything automatically unless you've set up a reminder).
  • To send you notifications about missed calls, job reminders, and payment follow-ups that you've configured.
  • To process card payments through Stripe when you choose to offer card payment to your customers.
  • To improve the Service through aggregate analytics.
  • To contact you about important account or service changes.

3. Where your data lives

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — Buildlogg is an offline-first app. Your jobs, customers, and quotes are stored locally on your phone in an encrypted database. This is why the app works without signal.
  • In the cloud (Supabase, EU/UK region) — when you have an internet connection, your data syncs to our Supabase instance hosted in the EU. This backs up your data and lets you use the Service across devices. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS.

We do not transfer your data outside the EU/UK. We do not use third-party analytics providers that transfer data to the US.

4. Who we share with

We do not sell your data. We do not share your customer list, job details, or pricing with anyone. The only third parties we work with are:

  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting (EU region).
  • Stripe — card payment processing. Stripe handles card data directly under their own PCI-DSS compliance. We never see or store your customers' card numbers.
  • PostHog — product analytics (EU-hosted, self-hosted instance).
  • WhatsApp / SMS gateways — when you choose to send a quote or message, it's sent through the relevant platform. We don't read or store the content of messages you send.

We will only disclose your data to law enforcement if legally compelled to do so, and only the minimum required.

5. Your customers' data

When you enter a customer's phone number, name, or address into Buildlogg, you are the data controller for that information. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to store your customers' contact details (this is standard for running a business). Buildlogg processes this data on your behalf as a data processor.

If a customer asks you to delete their details, you can remove them from Buildlogg at any time by deleting the customer record in the app.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of all the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent — for any processing based on consent (e.g. analytics), you can withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@buildlogg.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your data from our servers within 30 days. Your local device data is deleted immediately when you uninstall the app.

If you stop using the Service but don't delete your account, we'll keep your data for 12 months in case you return, then automatically delete it.

8. Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS.
  • Your local device database is encrypted.
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with secure session management.
  • Access to our backend systems is restricted and logged.
  • We do not store card numbers — Stripe handles all card data under their PCI-DSS compliance.

No system is 100% secure. If we ever suffer a data breach, we'll notify affected users and the ICO within 72 hours, as required by law.

9. Cookies

The Buildlogg website uses minimal cookies — only what's needed for the page to function and for aggregate analytics via PostHog. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party ad networks. The PWA app does not use cookies at all.

10. Children

Buildlogg is a business tool for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us and we'll delete the account.

11. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we'll notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date above. We won't make changes that reduce your rights without explicit consent.

Questions about your data?

Email us at hello@buildlogg.com — we're a small team and a real person will read your message.
